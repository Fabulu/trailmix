ENEMY SPRITE ART BRIEF — Trail Mix
======================================
Top-down roguelike, 256x192 NDS. 4bpp indexed color, transparent BG.
Clean pixel art. No anti-aliasing, no dithering. 1px dark outline on all bodies.
You choose the creature designs. These are characters, not geometric shapes.

FORMAT: Each file is a 2-frame horizontal strip (frame 1 | frame 2).
Sizes: small=8px (16x8 PNG), medium=16px (32x16 PNG), large=24px (48x24 PNG), boss=32px (64x32 PNG).

SIZE DIFFERENCES: Small = minimal detail, just silhouette + color. Medium = full detail, readable features. Large = same character, bulkier proportions, extra detail (scars, armor, thicker limbs).
